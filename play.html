The Evergreen Kingdom

About The Game:

The Evergreen Kingdom is an interactive story that revolves around the evironment as being the story's main theme. You play as the rightful king who is tasked with making decisions that can either positvely or negatively affect the outcome of the game. The ideal outcome would be keeping your kingdom as healthy as possible in terms of clean enviroment and healthy people. There is also a stats system that has four categories: LP (loyalty points), EP(enviroment points), DP(dilopmatic points), RP(resources points), and each carries its own importance in the story-line. You are to make the right choices to keep these points above the minimum or your kingdom will fail.


At the start of the game, you will be introduced to the stats system that has detailed explanations about what each stat category means.

There are two choices at every turn of the story and you are to make the most ideal choice for your kingdom. Keep in mind that you have to look after your stats points to avoid any of them going below the minimum threshold

There are usually no right or wrong answers, just pick the most ideal choice. Some might affect your stats points and some not.
This is your stats screen and you can and should check your kingdom stats before making decision.
