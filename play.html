The Evergreen Kingdom

About The Game:

The Evergreen Kingdom is an interactive story that revolves around the evironment as being the story's main theme. You play as the rightful king who is tasked with making decisions that can either positvely or negatively affect the outcome of the game. The ideal outcome would be keeping your kingdom as healthy as possible in terms of clean enviroment and healthy people. There is also a stats system that has four categories: LP (loyalty points), EP(enviroment points), DP(dilopmatic points), RP(resources points), and each carries its own importance in the story-line. You are to make the right choices to keep these points above the minimum or your kingdom will fail.

The young king’s mission is to control his kingdom after his father’s death due to the disease caused by climate change. There will be climate change problems arise at the beginning of the game. If the player chooses the environment-friendly route, they will possibly end up with a good ending where the kingdom achieves peace and clean environment. If the player chooses the opposite route, they will for sure end up with a bad ending such as watching the kingdom conquered or the king being executed. Additionally, we included special features to make this game more interacting which are status bars. Based on each decision the player makes, the status of loyalty points, environmental points, diplomatic points, and resources points will change. In general, the player wants to keep the loyalty points and resources points decent to order the people in the kingdom to perform a certain action such as fighting without having too much risk. If these two stats are low, the chances of losing fights and the kingdom being disordered are high. The player wants to maintain diplomatic points in order to establish peace and avoid fights with other kingdoms. Environmental point is crucial because it determines the success of the king ruling the kingdom overall. Having high environmental points would guarantee a good ending for the king.

The game focuses on situational decision making based on the stats the player currently have. The climate change plays a big role on whether the king succeeds or fails to govern his kingdom. We included information about vector-borne disease that is caused by climate change, which kills the young king’s father. We wanted to let the player know that climate change will be one of the main themes in this game. We achieved this more when we put a situation that involves climate change such as whether or not to pause the factories. The key of the game lies in here. If the player chooses to not pause the factories, the commoners’ health conditions will be affected badly due to the dirty air and rising temperature, and that effect will carry on until the end of the game. We didn’t meet our intent the way we want it to be. If we included more situations involving climate change, the climate change theme and its bond to the game will be more clear to the player.

Game Play:

At the start of the game, you will be introduced to the stats system that has detailed explanations about what each stat category means.

There are two choices at every turn of the story and you are to make the most ideal choice for your kingdom. Keep in mind that you have to look after your stats points to avoid any of them going below the minimum threshold

There are usually no right or wrong answers, just pick the most ideal choice. Some might affect your stats points and some not.
This is your stats screen and you can and should check your kingdom stats before making decision.
